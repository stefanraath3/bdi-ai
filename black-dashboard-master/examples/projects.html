Projects & KPI's Context

This "Projects & KPI's" page is designed to provide an in-depth view of the ongoing projects, their status, and the Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) associated with them. The main sections include:

  • Overall Project Completion: A visual representation of the cumulative progress across all projects, enabling easy tracking of overall completion status.
  • Active Projects: Details of individual projects, including their names and current completion percentages, to offer a granular view of each project's progress.
  • Additional Details with Tabs: This section delves into specific areas of interest such as Inspections & Audits, Employee Trainings, Stakeholder Meetings, and Employee Performance. Each tab provides relevant data and information tailored to that specific area.

Together, these sections give managers and executives a practical glimpse into the status and health of their projects and associated KPIs. The design aims to present information in a concise, easy-to-understand manner, ensuring that key decisions can be made effectively and efficiently.
